Frame Materials

Holloway Eyewear lets you choose the frame material that would best fit your fashion and lifestyle. Plastic frames come in a wide variety of colours and provide a lightweight solution, while corrosion-resistant titanium offers durability and flexibility. You can never go wrong with a wooden frame, as it is extremely customisable and great for highlighting your artistic and pro-nature side. Our bespoke frames are handmade using materials that will give comfort and a perfect, snug fit to young professionals and models like you.

We know how thrilling it is to level up your wardrobe with a new, customised eyewear. But first, we recommend looking at the properties and benefits of the frame materials before we start creating the design.

Plastic

Plastic frames are your best option if you are looking for thick and vibrant styles. They have a lightweight and durable construction that makes them comfortable for everyday wear. They are resistant to chemical corrosion which means that they do not corrode due to sweat.

Wood

Our wooden frames undergo a special layering technique that takes up several hours, giving them a strong and durable finish. Each of them is topped with an extra layer of bleach coating to make them resistant to scratches and accidental spills. Wooden frames are extremely customisable and gives out a bare and artistic look. This frame material is a good option if you want to enhance your authentic look without drawing away the attention from your outfit.

Holloway

Premium Acetate

Unlike single-colored plastic frames, premium acetate frames consist of layers of colors and are much more malleable. They are equipped with a metal core for easy and long-lasting adjustments. Its excellent elasticity is what makes it a popular choice.
